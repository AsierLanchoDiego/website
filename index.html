Both unordered and ordered lists come in different flavours. You can add some attributes to the tags to modify the appearance of your list. By default. Take a look:

ORDERED LISTS: by default, type="1" is used for these, which produces a list where all the items are numbered. Here are the other variants:

  1. type="A" > The list items will be numbered with uppercase letters
  1. type="a" > The list items will be numbered with lowercase letters
  1. type="I"> The list items will be numbered with uppercase roman numbers
  1. type="i" > The list items will be numbered with lowercase roman numbers
